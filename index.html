About

Beau’s Bonbons began in 2008 as a small shop selling homemade candy. Today we still make homemade candy, but we have 6 different locations in 3 different cities. And the best part—we now ship our delicious homemade goodies worldwide!

Everything is made in-store using fresh, natural ingredients and everything is packaged on the spot. We’ve grown to include a wide variety of candies and sweets that are all unique and delicious. There’s something here for everyone!
